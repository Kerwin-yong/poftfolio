Profit Model

Diversified revenue streams that create win-win value for both users and the platform

Simulated Trading Commissions

Reward promoters and active users with virtual incentives or physical gifts for bringing in new users through simulated trading. Earn referral commissions from brokers on successful user account openings.

Contract Trading Commissions

Revenue is shared between the platform, exchanges, and brokers based on user trading fees. A performance-based commission may also be collected from profitable users.

Copy Trading Commissions

When followers earn profits from copy trading, the platform shares a portion with expert traders. Referrers also earn tiered rewards from the profits of referred users.
